# Second Brain Setup Guide

A simple and powerful way to organize your ideas, documents, notes, and tasks using Tiago Forte's PARA Method.

Created by PM Researcher

PARA stands for:

- Projects: Active outcomes

- Areas: Ongoing responsibilities

- Resources: Reference material

- Archives: Completed or dormant content

Use this method to create a digital system that clears your mind and supports focused, creative

work.

## What is PARA?

- PARA is a simple method to organize your digital life into four categories:
- Projects: Active goals with clear outcomes

- Areas: Ongoing responsibilities or roles
- Resources: Knowledge, tools, or references you may need later
- Archives: Completed or inactive materials you want to keep

## Folder Setup Instructions

1. Create a main folder called 'Second Brain'
2. Inside it, create four subfolders:
  - Projects
  - Areas
  - Resources
  - Archives
3. Use these folders in cloud storage (Google Drive, Dropbox), Notion, or local files

## Sample Folder Structure

```
Second Brain
├── Projects
│   ├── Launch Podcast
│   ├── Plan Japan Trip
├── Areas
│   ├── Health
│   ├── Finances
├── Resources
│   ├── Book Notes
│   ├── Cooking Tips
├── Archives
    ├── Past Projects
    ├── Old Notes
```

## Tips for Maintaining Your PARA System

- Review Projects weekly and move completed ones to Archives
- Check Areas monthly to update or maintain habits
- Keep Resources searchable and easy to scan
- Avoid over-tagging. PARA thrives on simplicity
